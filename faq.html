Q: Why emojis and pictograms for the bus info?

A: It's better than reading two blobs of words (before 0.0.4). Also that means I have less strings to translate to French to accommodate the Loi 101.

Credits to KTon for helping out with the design.

Q: Maps outside Montreal are not loading for me? / Why am I not subject to OpenStreetMap's privacy policy?

A: All the map tiles (images) are proxied through the backend, meaning the web server will access OpenStreetMap on your behalf. Your data (Like IP addresses, browser, etc.) are NOT provided to them, meaning you're not subject to their privacy policy (Hooray for privacy!). However, to prevent overuse, maps outside Montreal (as defined by me) will not be loaded.

Q: Any future plans?
